# Red Pasta Sauce

## Ingredients
| Qty           | Thing
|---------------|-------
| 30 oz         | tomato sauce
| 15 oz         | tomato paste
| 1 lb          | ground beef
| 1/2           | medium onion
| 2 cloves      | garlic
| 1 tbsp, 1 tsp | oregano
| 1 tbsp        | basil
| 1 dash        | thyme

## Steps
1. Chop onion
1. Brown meat with onion
1. Drain meat
1. Add other ingredients
1. Simmer for at least a half hour (now's a good time to cook the pasta)
